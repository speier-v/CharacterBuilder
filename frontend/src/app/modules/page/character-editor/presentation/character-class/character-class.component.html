Character Class

A class represents your character's skills, abilities, and role in the adventure. Each class offers a unique set of talents and a specific playstyle that defines how your character can contribute to the group.

For example, the Fighter excels in physical combat, wielding weapons and wearing armor to protect themselves and deal damage effectively. They are versatile warriors capable of standing at the front lines.

The Rogue, on the other hand, specializes in stealth and cunning, using agility and intelligence to outsmart opponents, sneak through shadows, and deliver devastating sneak attacks.

Finally, the Wizard harnesses the power of arcane magic, casting spells to manipulate the battlefield, deal damage from a distance, or even assist allies.

Choosing a class is a crucial step in creating your character, as it determines your abilities, playstyle, and contributions to the adventuring party.
